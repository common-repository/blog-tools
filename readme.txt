== Description ==

 * This Plugin 'Blog_Tools' views the 'Post views' and 'Last Viewed-at time' just below the Title of the Post/Page.
 * This simple Plugin Helps the Admins to get the Info about the Views till date of various posts on the Blog in a form of Table Chart.
 * This may help them to Judge their various Authors and may also get the Idea of the tastes of their readers.
 * The Plugin views the 'Post views' and 'Last View at time' just below the Title of the Post/Page.
 * In the Admin area(Settings->Blog_tools), It shows the tables for Posts' Views along with the Author Name, Last View at, and IP of the last  
 * viewer

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
2. Search for 'Blog Tools'
3. Activate Blog_Tools from your Plugins page.

= From WordPress.org =

1. Download Blog_Tools
2. Upload the 'Blog_tools' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
3. Activate Blog_tools from your Plugins page.
